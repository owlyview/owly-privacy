📋 개인정보처리방침 (한국어)

1. 수집하는 정보

Owly View가 직접 수집하는 정보는 최소한이며, 일부 정보는 제3자 광고 파트너에 의해 수집될 수 있습니다 (제4조 참조).

Owly View가 직접 수집:

  • Firebase 익명 인증 ID: P2P 세션 식별용 무작위 ID. 실제 신원과 연결되지 않습니다.
  • 익명 채팅 메시지 및 커뮤니티 게시글: Firebase Firestore에 저장되며, 72시간 후 자동 삭제됩니다 (선별된 공지 및 인기글 Top 3 제외).
  • 닉네임 (선택): 커뮤니티 게시판용. 기기 내에만 저장됩니다.

수집하지 않는 정보:

  • 실명, 이메일, 전화번호 등 개인 식별 정보
  • 아기의 영상/음성 (P2P로만 전송, 서버에 도달하지 않음)
  • 건강, 금융, 연락처 데이터

2. 영상 및 음성 데이터

  • 허브 폰과 뷰어 폰 간 영상/음성은 WebRTC P2P 직접 연결로 전송됩니다.
  • 영상이나 음성은 우리 서버에 저장되거나 전송되거나 접근되지 않습니다.
  • 이벤트 감지 시 (울음, 비명, 기침, 얼굴 미감지 등) 15초 영상이 사용자의 로컬 기기에만 저장됩니다. 어디에도 업로드되지 않습니다.
  • 감지 기록 (이벤트 로그)은 기기 내에만 저장되며, 어떤 서버로도 전송되지 않습니다.

📋 Privacy Policy (English)

1. Information We Collect

Owly View collects minimal information directly. Some additional data may be collected by our third-party advertising partner (see Section 4).

Directly collected by Owly View:

  • Firebase Anonymous Authentication ID: A randomly generated identifier used solely as a P2P session token. Not linked to your real identity.
  • Anonymous chat messages and community posts: Stored on Firebase Firestore. Automatically deleted after 72 hours, except curated notices and Top 3 popular posts.
  • Nickname (optional): Used for the community board only. Stored on your device.

Not collected:

  • Real name, email, phone number, or any personal identifying information
  • Video or audio of your baby (transmitted P2P only and never reach our servers)
  • Health, financial, or contact data

2. Video and Audio Data

  • Video and audio between the Hub phone and Viewer phone are transmitted via direct WebRTC P2P connection.
  • No video or audio is stored on, transmitted through, or accessible to our servers.
  • When events are detected (cry, scream, cough, face-miss, etc.), 15-second clips may be saved only on your local device. They are not uploaded anywhere.
  • Detection history (event logs) is stored only on your device and is never sent to any server.

4. Third-Party Services and Advertising

(a) Google Firebase

  • Firebase Authentication: Provides anonymous user IDs. No personal info.
  • Firebase Firestore: Stores anonymous chat/posts and is used for the initial WebRTC signaling handshake (exchanging connection offers, not media).
  • Reference: Firebase Privacy Policy

(b) Google AdMob (Advertising)

AdMob serves advertisements within the App. To do so, AdMob may collect:

  • Advertising identifier (IDFA on iOS, Advertising ID on Android)
  • Approximate (coarse) location data
  • Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks)
  • Device information (model, OS version)
On iOS: The App Tracking Transparency (ATT) prompt is shown before any tracking-based advertising. If you decline, only non-personalized ads will be shown. The core monitoring features of the App work fully regardless of your ATT choice.
  • Reference: Google Privacy Policy
  • Reference: AdMob Data Disclosure

5. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. Users are parents and guardians. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children. The App is used to monitor an infant via two devices owned by the parent/guardian; the infant is not a "user" of the App in any data-collection sense.

6. Permissions

  • Camera: Video capture on the Hub phone
  • Microphone: Sound detection (cry/scream/cough) and audio transmission
  • Bluetooth: Optional connection to a temperature/humidity sensor (Xiaomi LYWSD03MMC)
  • Notifications: Event detection alerts on the Viewer phone
  • Local Network (iOS): WebRTC P2P discovery on local Wi-Fi
  • Photo Library / Storage: Saving event recordings on your device

All permissions are requested with the user's explicit consent at the time of first use. You may revoke any permission at any time via your device settings.

  • Withdraw consent at any time
